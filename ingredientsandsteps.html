How to Bake Cookies the Right Way

List of ingredients:

  • 1 stick of butter
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 12 oz. bag chocolate chips
  • 2 1/4 cups all purpose flour
  • 3/4 teaspoon baking soda


Steps to making the perfect cookies...

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Mix butter and eggs in a large bowl.
  3. Do NOT completely melt the butter because then your chocolate chips will melt from the heat.
  4. Next, mix flour, baking soda, and sugar. Slowly add to egg and butter using a hand mixer.
  5. Finally, mix in the chocolate chips with a spoon, NOT the mixer.
  6. Place cookie dough balls on greased pan at your desired size. Leave enough space between each cookie dough ball because the cookies will flatten and spread.
